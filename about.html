Company Overview

Marghob Heray is a fully women-owned agribusiness established in 2023 in Herat Province, Afghanistan, with a strong commitment to women's economic empowerment and sustainable agriculture.

Specializing in processing, drying, grading, cleaning, and packaging premium-quality saffron with improved hygienic standards and structured quality control systems.

Our Mission

To enhance value addition, strengthen competitiveness, and ensure compliance with international food safety and export standards while promoting women's economic empowerment and sustainable agriculture.

Contributing to increased income generation for producers and improved market access for premium Afghan saffron globally.

  • Standardized quality control & traceability
  • International export compliance
  • Women-centered income opportunities
